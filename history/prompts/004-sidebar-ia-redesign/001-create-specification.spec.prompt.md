# Specification: Sidebar IA Redesign

## Feature Overview

Created comprehensive specification for redesigning the sidebar information architecture of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. The specification focuses on organizing content by modules/weeks with consistent naming and utility links for setup, hardware requirements, and assessments.

## Key Elements

- **User Story 1 (P1)**: Module-based navigation with hierarchical organization
- **User Story 2 (P2)**: Quick access to utility resources
- **User Story 3 (P3)**: Consistent navigation experience
- **Functional Requirements**: 9 specific requirements covering hierarchy, naming, accessibility, and link integrity
- **Success Criteria**: 9 measurable outcomes focused on usability and accessibility

## Quality Assurance

- Edge cases identified and documented
