# Introduction

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. This comprehensive guide covers the fundamental concepts, practical implementations, and cutting-edge research in the field of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## About this Textbook

This textbook is designed for students, researchers, and practitioners interested in understanding and developing physical AI systems and humanoid robots. We cover topics ranging from basic kinematics and dynamics to advanced machine learning approaches for robot control.
