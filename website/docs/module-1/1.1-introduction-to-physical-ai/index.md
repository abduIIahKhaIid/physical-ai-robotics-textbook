# 1.1 Physical AI Foundations & Concepts

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, students will be able to:
- Define Physical AI and distinguish it from traditional AI approaches
- Explain the concept of embodied intelligence
- Describe the historical evolution of Physical AI
- Identify current applications and future potential

## Topics Covered

### Day 1: What is Physical AI?
- Defining Physical AI vs. Digital AI
- Key characteristics and differences
- Historical context and development

### Day 2: Differences Between Digital and Physical AI Systems
- Computational vs. physical constraints
- Real-time processing requirements
- Interaction with physical environment

### Day 3: Historical Context and Evolution
- Early developments in embodied AI
- Key milestones and breakthroughs
- Current state of the field

### Day 4: Current Applications and Future Potential
- Industrial applications
- Service robotics
- Research frontiers
- Future trends and possibilities

### Day 5: Review and Practical Exercise
- Weekly quiz preparation
- Hands-on simulation exercise
- Discussion forum participation
