# Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Concepts

This section introduces cutting-edge approaches to artificial intelligence in humanoid robotics, where vision, language, and action are combined into unified systems. Understanding VLA models is essential for creating intelligent humanoid robots capable of complex tasks.

## Topics Covered

- Vision-Language-Action model architectures
- Embodied AI integration principles
- Practical applications of VLA in robotics
- Connection between perception and action

## Learning Objectives

After completing this section, you should be able to:
- Understand the architecture and functioning of VLA models
- Explain how embodied AI differs from traditional AI approaches
- Identify practical applications of VLA in humanoid robotics
- Design systems that connect visual perception with language understanding and physical action
